How To Make a Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwhich

  1. Grab Two Slices of bread, some peanut butter and jelly
  2. Lather 2 tbsp of peanut butter on one of the two slices of bread
  3. Lather 2 tbsp of jelly on the 2nd of the two slices of bread
  4. combine both slices of bread making sure peanut butter and jelly side touch
